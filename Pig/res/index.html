How to play

  • Local play

    In local play, you play against another player on the same device.

    Enter the two player's names in the 'Player Names' section.

    Press 'Local Play' on the main menu to begin a game.

  • Network play

    Network play requires two devices that can connect together over a network.

    Both devices must have the game installed, and for Bluetooth or WiFi to be enabled.

    Choose 'network play' from the main menu to start searching for another player.

    Once another player is found, press connect, and the game will begin.

  • Game Rules

    Pig is a game played with two dice. The object of the game is to reach 100 points before your opponent.

    Each player take turns rolling the two dice. If no die shows a '1', the player adds the face value of the dice to their round score.

    If either die shows a '1', the player scores 0 for their round, and their turn ends.

    If both dice show '1', then the player's total score is reduced to 0, and their turn ends.

    After each roll, a player may choose to roll again, or 'hold'.

    If a player holds, their round score is added to their total score and their turn ends.

    The game ends when one player reaches a total of 100 points.
